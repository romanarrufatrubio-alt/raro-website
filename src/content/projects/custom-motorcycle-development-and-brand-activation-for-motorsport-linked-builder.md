Within a custom motorcycle builder closely connected to the professional motor sport ecosystem, we worked at the intersection of product, brand and performance.

The project involved the design and development of bespoke motorcycles for a highly specific audience, including athletes and professional riders. Each build required aligning visual identity, technical configuration and riding intent—treating every motorcycle as a product shaped by both performance needs and personal expression.

Beyond the workshop, we contributed to defining how the brand showed up in a sports-driven context. Through targeted digital marketing and branding initiatives, the focus was on positioning the builder within a performance-oriented audience, reinforcing credibility through real connections to racing culture.

Strategic collaborations with local businesses and motor sport-related partners helped strengthen community ties, expand visibility and increase market penetration without compromising authenticity.

The result was hands-on experience in shaping motorcycles and brands simultaneously—where product decisions, technical specifications and storytelling worked together to support a clear positioning within the motor sport-influenced custom scene.
