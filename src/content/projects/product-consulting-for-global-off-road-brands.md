Within a top-tier global design and strategy agency specialized in the motorcycle industry, we worked on the strategic definition of product portfolios for some of the most influential off-road and rally brands in the world.

The project focused on aligning brand positioning with real market demand, translating consumer insights, global trends and competitive landscapes into clear product decisions.

Our work spanned brand assessment, portfolio logic and long-term positioning across multiple brands operating in the off-road segment.

Through market research, benchmarking, trend analysis and target customer evaluation, we not only defined which products should exist and where they should compete, but also outlined how those products should be configured, translating market expectations and rider needs into high-level product specifications.

The outcome was a product strategy grounded in data, brand clarity and rider reality supporting informed decision-making for future off-road and rally line-ups at a global scale, ensuring launching a motorcycle ready to fill the gaps.
