We are currently exploring a product leadership role within the electric mobility division of a global consumer electronics and home technology brand.

The project focuses on defining product vision, portfolio logic and market fit for electric mobility solutions—bridging consumer expectations, industrial capabilities and fast-evolving mobility trends.

As part of this process, on-site collaboration with manufacturing partners in China plays a key role in shaping the final product—aligning design intent, technical feasibility and production realities.

While the scope and final direction are still taking shape, the opportunity sits at the intersection of product strategy, innovation and scalable consumer mobility.

Details will remain under wraps for now.
